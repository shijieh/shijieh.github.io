* [cite_start]Developed a novel numerical method to efficiently model power takeoff devices (turbines)[cite: 29].

### Coastal Resilience & Coral Reefs
* [cite_start]Developed a validated 3D numerical model using **OpenFOAM** to simulate hydrodynamics in idealized barrier reef systems[cite: 34].
* [cite_start]Identified key coastal erosion mechanisms and proposed nature-based solutions, including coral reef restoration and artificial reef crests[cite: 35, 36].
* [cite_start]Currently collaborating with the **Hawaii Department of Transportation (HDOT)** to predict long-term shoreline evolution for hazard mitigation.

### Advanced Sediment Transport Modeling
* [cite_start]Developing two-way coupling sediment transport models that capture dynamic interactions between sediment movement and hydrodynamics[cite: 40].
